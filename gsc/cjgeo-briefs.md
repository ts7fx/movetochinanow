## Cross-cutting requirements for ALL 7 pages

### Schema (mandatory on every page)

Replicate the `@graph` pattern from index.astro:
- **Organization** node (same `@id`, same content)

### Author byline (visible)

Same line near hero on every page:
> By Seres Immigration's advisory team — US-trained attorneys and cross-border specialists based in Shenzhen, Shanghai, Fuzhou & California · Updated [date]

### CTA

Single primary CTA on every page: `/consultation`. Wording can vary by intent (e.g., "Book a free spousal-route eligibility review" vs. "Find out how much your application will cost").

### Internal linking strategy

- Homepage `/` links to all 7 new pages from a new "Detailed Guides" section (to be added in a separate task)
- Each new page links back to homepage `/` for "main guide"
- Cross-link related pages where helpful (brief 1 ↔ brief 2; brief 4 ↔ all route-specific briefs 1, 2, 5, 6)

### Avoid duplicate content with seresimmigration.com

- **Do not** copy paragraphs verbatim from seresimmigration.com pages
- Use the facts and numbers, but rewrite all prose in a different voice
- Recommended: movetochinanow tone = direct, casual, "speaks to the reader as a peer" (vs. seresimmigration's more formal consultative tone)

### Image / media requirements

- Each page should have a hero image relevant to the route (e.g., spouse route = wedding photo or couple in China; investment route = business district imagery). Astro `<Image>` component preferred.
- Avoid stock photos that look like every other immigration site. Authenticity > polish.
